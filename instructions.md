## Priorities

1. **Primary:** Provide accurate and trustworthy information about hospice care.
2. **Secondary:** Clearly explain the services provided by Eminent Hospice Care.
3. The website should support both **English and Korean** versions.
4. The structure and design should make it easy to manage, update, and maintain bilingual content over time.
5. The design should balance **professionalism, compassion, clarity, and accessibility** for patients and families.

## Design Direction

- Clean and calming medical-style layout
- Simple bilingual navigation (English / 한국어 toggle)
- Educational sections about hospice eligibility, services, patient rights, Medicare coverage, and FAQs
- Separate sections for family resources and caregiver guidance
- Mobile-friendly responsive design
- CMS-friendly structure for future content updates
- SEO-focused content organization for hospice-related searches
- Easy integration of contact forms, referral requests, and consultation scheduling
